FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How do I sign up for the sharing mobility app? You can sign up by downloading the app from the App Store or Google Play Store and following the registration process.
What types of vehicles are available for sharing on the app? The app offers a variety of vehicles including cars, scooters, bicycles, and more for users to share.
How can I pay for my rides on the app? You can pay for your rides using credit/debit cards, mobile wallets, or other payment methods integrated into the app.
Is there a membership fee to use the sharing mobility app? There is no membership fee to use the app. You only pay for the rides or services you use.
How can I report an issue with a vehicle or a ride? You can report any issues with vehicles or rides through the app by contacting customer support or using the in-app reporting feature.
